Afghan Proverbs

Wisdom from the heart of Afghanistan

Afghan proverbs carry centuries of wisdom passed down through generations. These sayings reflect the values, humor, and cultural richness of the Afghan people.

تا مرد سخن نگفته باشد، عیب و هنرش نهفته باشد

“A man's faults and virtues are hidden until he speaks.”

قطره قطره دریا می‌شود

“Drop by drop, a river is made.”
